About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineer specializing in CAD modeling, finite element analysis, and design optimization. My expertise bridges theoretical analysis with practical engineering solutions through rigorous simulation and iterative design refinement. I focus on static structural analysis, nonlinear FEA, topology optimization, and design for manufacturing (DFM/DFMA). My workflow integrates parametric modeling in Siemens NX, mesh convergence studies, material selection, and GD&T to achieve optimal strength-to-weight ratios while ensuring manufacturing feasibility. I am a December 2025 graduate from the University of Wisconsin-Madison with a Master's in Mechanical Engineering, committed to launching my career in advanced mechanical design and structural analysis.
