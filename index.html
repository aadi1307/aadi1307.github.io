About Me

I'm a Mechanical Engineer specializing in advanced CAD modeling, finite element analysis, and design optimization. My expertise lies in bridging theoretical analysis with practical engineering solutions through rigorous simulation and iterative design refinement.

I specialize in static structural analysis, nonlinear FEA, topology optimization, and design for manufacturing. My approach integrates analytical methods with design intent, leveraging FEA to validate structural integrity, topology optimization to achieve optimal material distribution, and design optimization workflows to balance performance constraints with manufacturing feasibility.

Each project in my portfolio demonstrates technical precision in CAD modeling, rigorous analysis through finite element methods, and systematic optimization. From initial concept through validation, I employ a comprehensive engineering workflow: parametric modeling in Siemens NX, mesh convergence studies, stress analysis with nonlinear material behavior, and iterative optimization to achieve superior strength to weight ratios.

I am a December 2025 graduate from the University of Wisconsin Madison with a Master's degree in Mechanical Engineering, and I'm committed to launching my professional career in advanced mechanical design and structural analysis.
